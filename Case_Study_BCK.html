About The Project

BCK United is a nonprofit focused on empowering young leaders to bring positive change and build a healthy, inclusive community in Baldwin City, Kansas. This non-profit organization wants to expand their reach by building a website that can provide helpful resources, hold events and activities, and provide a place for the users to learn and join in their cause.

Solution

Our team has created a website that specifically targets adolescents and young adults in the greater community of Baldwin City, KS. Through the research and design process we decided that there should be five user-friendly pathways for a better experience.
